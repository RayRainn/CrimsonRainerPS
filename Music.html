Crimson Rainer’s Music Project

Crimson's evolution in metal instrumentals utilizes conflicting opposites. Based in Thousand Oaks, these hard and soft natured opposites give you a journey through his mind. Crimson’s music draws influence from Mick Gordon with the suspense and intense riffs along with 2 steps from hells light orchestral melodies giving Crimson’s visions a clear voice. Crimson has been honing his skills in guitar for 5 years and composing for 1 and a half years; with him pushing the dynamics to give you power and motivation. Crimson started musically through jamming over covers at a music club at university and watching how it turned into something different, opening up his world, with his love of rock , metal and orchestral, he brought them together to create these epic tracks.

The themes Crimson Rainer follow are: Powerful/Epic/Dynamic/Melodic/Fired Up/Heavy
